Project Design

1. Project Overview

  1. This website is an online clothing store that serves as a project for my friend who recently graduated community college and wants to open a business. This won't function as a real online store, but will give my client an idea for what her actual store would look like in the future.
  2. The intended users for this website are plus size men and women.
  3. This website will have contents such as a homepage that includes info about the owner, two pages for women's dresses and jeans, one page for men's jeans, and a news page about upcoming clothes. There are five pages in total.
